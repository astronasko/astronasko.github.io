Pleased to meet you. I am a fourth-year MSci student in University College London with a strong technical background in system programming. I aim to contribute to society as a scientist, and my long-term objective is to develop a multitude of astrophysical tools that can be used with diverse sources of data.

My academic records have been excellent so far, and I am on track to graduate with a First-Class Honours degree. Independently from university, a fellow undergraduate and I made a discovery in the field of cataclysmic variable stars only recently. I have two other observational contributions: a spectroscopic confirmation of a supernova and observations of a possible dwarf nova. I happen to be currently involved in several projects of various nature, in which I contribute to the algorithmic/modelling line of work at least.
